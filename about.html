About Our School

INTRODUCTION


The School “Maryam Memorial Institute Pandithpora” functions under the supervision of Maryam Memorial Trust (MMT), Pandithpora. Maryam Memorial Trust (MMT) is a body of well learned human beings working on Volunteer basis, selflessly for the sake of public interest. The “TRUST” is a non profit, non commercial organization and has no connection with any political group. Maryam Memorial Trust (MMT) is a registered trust with the Government by court of law from 29 – 12 – 2007. Before the registration, a Managing Committee of the TRUST was approved and accepted by the Government vide Order No : 1257 DSEK of 2005 Dated: 11-06-2005. Second Managing Committee was approved by the Government in the year 2011 vide Order No: 1345 DSEK of 2011 Dt: 21-07-2011. The Third Managing Committee was approved by the Government in the year 2015 vide Order No: 1747 DSEK of 2015 Dt: 11-06-2015.

ESTABLISHMENT


Maryam Memorial Institute is purposely established to provide and promote the quality education and moral values as per the demand of the time. Established in April 1991 in the house of Haji Mohammad Shaban Bhat, Pandithpora on rent-less basis. In 1992, MMI was shifted to Bunpora Pandithpora in the house of Ghulam Mohammad Mir on rent basis. School was again shifted to the house of Khawaja Ghulam Rasool on rent basis for 2 years. In the meanwhile, in 1994, residents of Village Pandithpora provided Kahchari Land of 18 kanals and 03 malras to the School for construction and development of a School Building and premises. The same land was transferred to the Institution by the name “Maryam Memorial Institute” on 28 June 2006 by the order of Settlement Commissioner Kashmir, who in his presence got it entered in the Jamabandi Khasra No: 891 by the concerned Patwari.

REGISTRATION OF THE INSTITUTION


MMI is a Registered Private Institution, registered with the Jummu & Kashmir Board of School Education. MMI was registered upto to Middle level in the year 2004 vide Government Order No: 5620 DSEK of 2004 Dt.: 18-12-2004. The Institute was upgraded and registered to the Secondary level in the year 2006 vide Government Order No: 445 Edu of 2006 Dt.: 20-09-2006.

ESTABLISHMENT AND CONSTRUCTION

First School Building comprising of 10 rooms was constructed in 1995 with the help of Donations made from the Public. This building is now called “The Old Block”. In 1996, the plinth of “New Block” was laid down with a dimensions of 100'×50’. This building was upgraded to first floor in the year 2012 under the Contractship of “Raja Constructions” owned by Bashir Ahmad Malik and Mohammad Yousuf Sofi of Galoora. In 2004, Iqbal Memorial Trust Srinagar donated a building to the Institution , funded by Islamic Development Bank as “Laboratory Block” for promotion of Science as a subject on modern basis, wherein 3 big hall rooms, 3 side rooms and 3 washrooms are available. The block has a Well Equipped Science Laboratory. The Laboratory Block was handed over to Maryam Memorial Institute Pandithpora in 2005 by The Chairman Iqbal Memorial Trust, Srinagar. A four roomed washroom block was constructed in 2004. A 5 roomed sanitary block is constructed alongside for girl students exclusively. Another 4 roomed lavatory Block was constructed in 2020 The Institute has a Canteen Block in the premises for facility of Students and Staff. The first floor of the Canteen is reserved as residential apartment for the School Security Guard (Chowkidaar). ASSETS The Institution owns 3 School Buses. 3 kanals of land has been purchased by the Institute for the purpose of construction of Exclusive Girls Block. A set of copper utensils being purchased by the School and is given to the needy people on the rent basis, which is a good source of Income to the School. School has an adequate number of furniture in terms of chairs, tables, benches and tool are available in the School. A good quantity of furnishing is also available in the School. There is a Computer Lab well equipped with the Computers and other Electronic Gadgets. School is safe and fully equipped with the Fire Safety Gadgets and Equipment. School has its Electrical and Gas Heaters for winters and LPG Gas Stove with Gas Containers for Cooking Purposes. The premises of Maryam Memorial Institute is fenced with GI sheets. And the purchased 3 kanals land is partly fenced with GI sheets and partly with mettle wire.
