About Our School

Maryam Memorial Institute is a respected educational institution located in Tawheed Bagh, Pandithpora, Qaziabad, Kupwara. The school is committed to providing quality education, discipline, and moral values to students.

Our aim is to create a learning environment where students can achieve academic excellence while developing confidence, leadership, and good character.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide modern education along with strong ethical values so that students become responsible citizens and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

We believe every child has unique potential. Our vision is to help students grow intellectually, socially, and morally while preparing them for future success.

Quality Education

Experienced teachers and modern learning methods for academic excellence.

Discipline

Strong focus on discipline, punctuality, and respectful behavior.

Student Development

Encouraging creativity, leadership, sports, and overall personality growth.
